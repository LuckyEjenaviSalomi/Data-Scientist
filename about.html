About Me

Lucky Ejenavi Salomi is a trained Data Scientist, Data Analyst and Chartered Accountant with sound mastery of IT and automation.
He provides critical bridge needed to transform data input to actionable insights and decisions. His masterful array of skills provides solid footing for an unrivalled ability to deliver projects and tasks quickly and accurately in an agile and flexible manner. He brings with him extensive and deep domain knowledge in Finance and Accounting, budgeting, business planning, inventory control, general ledger, taxation, management accounting, cost accounting, bank reconciliation, financial reporting, management advisory and investment appraisal. His passion and focus involves uncovering hidden patterns in data and presenting them in easy-to-read visualization to support sound management decision. His work experience in Coca Cola Hellenic Bottling Company (CCHBC) exposed him to financial analysis, business planning, variance analysis, investment appraisal, payroll reconciliation, internal control, information systems, treasury & foreign exchange operation, accrual accounting, sales accounting, performance measurement and management and inventory control management. Specifically, he developed within a short span into a top-norch Finance Analyst in CCHBC where he was appointed to pioneer the establishment of a new Import Cost Control and Tracking Unit in the Finance Function. With a keen eye on details, analytical mind and boldness to challenge the status quo, he achieved savings of NGN26million per year from 2010 in VAT on imported raw and packaging materials with recognition for excellence from the Finance Director in 2010. He Successfully developed a working financial analysis tracker and system for logistics reimbursement claims backup from The Coca-Company Company. He is an associate member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria with four (4) Prize Awards recognition at the professional examinations level in 2007 and 2008. He is also a member of the Information Systems, Audit & Control Association based in the US.
